# NUMU — Vision

NUMU is a material company.

We design bio-engineered materials grown from natural fibers to replace synthetic foams and mineral fibers.

This is not a tech product.
This is a material system.

---

## Core Narrative

The materials shaping our spaces should no longer come from fossil chemistry.

What if materials were grown instead of manufactured?

The future of materials lies at the intersection of biology and engineering.

---

## Positioning

NUMU sits at the intersection of:
- biology
- architecture
- material science

The brand must feel:
- calm
- premium
- tactile
- architectural

Avoid:
- startup aesthetics
- biotech/pharma visuals
- AI/cyberpunk language

---

## Core Idea

A bio-engineered material platform centered around a “mycofoam” nucleus.

Performance is defined during growth, not added later.

The material can be tuned for:
- density
- porosity
- mechanical performance

---

## Platform Logic

Acoustics is the entry point.

The system expands into:
- insulation
- interior materials
- construction systems

NUMU is not a product.
It is a scalable material platform.

---

## Modes

The website operates in two modes:

1. Visitor (architects, designers, clients)
2. Investor (partners, venture)

The transition must feel like:
- entering another layer of the same system
- not a UI toggle

Investor mode is darker, more structured, more explicit.

Visitor mode is lighter, more sensory, more material-driven.

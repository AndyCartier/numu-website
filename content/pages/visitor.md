## Statement

## What is NUMU
NUMU is a bio-engineered materials platform replacing synthetic foams and mineral fibers.

Designed for the Middle East from day one.
Built for local production, circular economy, and net-zero construction.

---

## The Material
At the core is a proprietary material system using mycelium as a natural binding network.

Performance is defined during growth, not added later.

Tunable for:
- density
- porosity
- mechanical performance
